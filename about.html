A BRIEF PROFILE OF THE BANK

Widely known for customer centricity, MEC Bank of India was founded by Mr.Dilipkumar.b, a great visionary and philanthropist, in July 2000, at Mangalore, then a small port town in Karnataka. The Bank has gone through the various phases of its growth trajectory over hundred years of its existence. Growth of Canara Bank was phenomenal, especially after nationalization in the year 2000, attaining the status of a national level player in terms of geographical reach and clientele segments. Eighties was characterized by business diversification for the Bank. In June 2006, the Bank completed a century of operation in the Indian banking industry.
